Nestled on the legendary Park Street in the heart of Kolkata, Siamton Inn is a sanctuary of warmth, elegance, and genuine Bengali hospitality. We marry the timeless heritage of the City of Joy with the refined comforts of modern living.

Every corner whispers stories of this city — its culture, its history, its soul. Whether here for business or leisure, Siamton Inn promises an experience that feels personal, polished, and deeply Kolkata.
